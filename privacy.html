Privacy Policy

How DocFinder collects, uses, and protects your personal and health information.

Effective: April 2026
Contact: hello@docfinder.in
Governed by: Indian law

1. Introduction

DocFinder ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of every person who uses our platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

By using DocFinder — whether through our website, mobile app, or WhatsApp service — you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

We do NOT sell your personal data. We do NOT use your health information for advertising. Ever.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Name, mobile number, and email address when you register
  • Location (city, area, or pin code) to show nearby doctors
  • Health-related information such as symptoms you describe
  • Prescriptions, reports, or medical documents you upload to Health Records
  • Feedback and ratings you give to doctors

Information we collect automatically

  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Pages you visit and features you use within the app
  • Appointment booking history
  • Voice input if you use the AI voice feature (processed in real-time, not stored)

Information from third parties

  • Doctor details from NMC public registry for verification
  • Hospital and facility data from NHM Karnataka and ABDM public databases

3. How we use your information

  • To match you with the right doctor or hospital based on your symptoms and location
  • To send appointment confirmations and reminders via SMS and WhatsApp
  • To verify that doctors listed on DocFinder are MCI registered
  • To improve our AI symptom checker and language support
  • To send important service updates and safety notifications
  • To comply with legal obligations under Indian law

4. Health data — special protection

DocFinder handles health-related information with extra care. We follow these principles:

  • Health data is stored with encryption at rest and in transit
  • Health records you upload are only visible to you and doctors you explicitly share them with
  • We never share your health information with employers, insurers, or advertisers
  • You can delete your health records at any time from within the app
  • We retain health data only as long as you have an active account

5. Sharing your information

We share your information only in these limited situations:

  • With doctors you book appointments with — only the information needed for the consultation
  • With SMS and WhatsApp service providers to send appointment reminders
  • With government authorities if required by law or court order
  • With our technology partners who help operate the platform, under strict data protection agreements

All third-party partners are contractually required to protect your data and may not use it for any other purpose.

6. Data storage and security

  • All data stored on secure cloud servers with industry-standard encryption
  • All connections protected with HTTPS
  • Access to your personal data within our team is strictly limited and logged
  • Regular security reviews of our systems

If a data breach occurs that affects your information, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

7. Your rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@docfinder.in. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's privacy

DocFinder is not intended for use by children under 18 without parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has shared personal information with us, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page and notify you via the app or SMS.
