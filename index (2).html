Features

Everything a patient needs,
in one place

Built specifically for Karnataka — covering Bengaluru to the smallest village across all 31 districts.

🤖
AI in 10 languages
Voice search and AI chat in Kannada, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam and 5 more. Describe symptoms in your own language.
🏛️
Government hospitals
PHC, CHC, district hospitals, Ayushman Bharat empanelled facilities — free treatment information across Karnataka.
🆘
Emergency SOS
One tap to call 108 ambulance, find nearest ER, offline first aid guide, and share location with family instantly.
🔍
Symptom checker
Tell us what hurts — AI finds the right specialist for you. No need to know medical terms. Works in any language.
✅
MCI verified doctors
Every doctor verified against the NMC registry. Registration number displayed. Trust before you book.
💬
WhatsApp booking
No app download needed. Village users can find and book a doctor with a simple WhatsApp message in any language.
📄
Health records wallet
Upload and store prescriptions, reports, and test results securely. Share with any doctor in one tap.
🚌
Travel time, not distance
"18 min by auto, 35 min by bus" — not just kilometres. Real travel time for village users who rely on public transport.
📲
SMS + WhatsApp reminders
Automatic appointment reminders sent 24 hours and 1 hour before. No missed appointments. Works on any phone.
